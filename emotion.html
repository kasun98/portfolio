Emotion Analysis

An Innovative Approach to Detecting Emotions in Real-Time Video


Recognizing emotions in real-time has vast applications ranging from entertainment to mental health analysis. Utilizing the power of YOLOv8, Roboflow for dataset annotation, and Google Colab for model training, I developed a robust project to detect and display cumulative emotions from frames of any video or live webcam feed.

GitHub GitHub Repository

The Concept

The core idea is to leverage YOLOv8 for detecting emotions in real-time. By training a custom model on a carefully annotated dataset, the project aims to identify various emotions and display a cumulative pie chart of these emotions in live video feeds.

Dataset and Model Development

I used Roboflow to annotate a dataset of facial expressions, covering a wide range of emotions. The dataset was then used to train a YOLOv8 model on Google Colab. The training process involved fine-tuning the model over 100 epochs.

Development Stack

  • Ultralytics YOLOV8: For training the emotion detection model.
  • Ultralytics Analytics: For rendering the realtime results.
  • Roboflow: For dataset annotation and management.
  • Google Colab: For training the model on custom data.
  • OpenCV and Matplotlib: For displaying real-time results and visualizations.

Limitations and Challenges

Despite its impressive capabilities, the project does have some limitations:

  • Dataset Quality: The accuracy of emotion detection is highly dependent on the quality and diversity of the training dataset.
  • Real-time Performance: Processing high-resolution video in real-time requires significant computational resources.
  • Limited Dataset: Due to the time-consuming nature of labeling, the model was trained on a limited dataset, which affected its overall performance. More extensive data collection and labeling are needed to improve the model's accuracy.

Conclusion

This project showcases the potential of combining YOLOv8 with real-time video processing to detect and analyze emotions. The resulting application can be utilized in various fields, providing valuable insights and enhancing user experience through real-time emotion recognition.
